Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Casual wear seems to be what people wear. You may see a tie or two but most wear that which they wear to work or school.

When should I arrive?

We would recommend that you arrive at about 15 minutes before the worship service or program (8:45 AM). This will give you time to get something to drink, find your way around and locate a seat.

What type of service will I experience?

Our worship services are blended in music. The teaching is Bible based and is presented in a way that makes scripture relevant to our daily lives. During each Sunday morning service, "Communion" is offered to all Christians present. Communion is also called "The Lord's Supper". It's a time when we can take a small piece of bread and a small cup of juice, to eat and drink, to remember Jesus' death on the cross. This time in the service is a time of personal worship that each Christian may experience as they choose.

How long does a Sunday morning service last?

The worship leaders and the preacher generally plan a service of about one hour and that is usually what happens. However, we have had services last a little longer and some shorter. We try not to allow the clock to be a big factor in our planing but we do respect everyone's family time.
