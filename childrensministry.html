Children's Ministry

We offer fun lessons in a youthful environment for children starting from birth until 4th grade.

Children's Welcome Center

  • We have an area designed just for our kids.
  • When you arrive, you will first see our "Children's Welcome Center". The staff check-in counter will receive information about your child to help us better care for them and keep them safe as you experience adult worship.
  • The check-in counter is where you can give the attendant information about your child.
  • Following service, you can pick your child up back at the Children's Welcome Center.

Fun Lessons

  • Your children will be led and taught by volunteer staff who will present fun lessons in a youthful environment.
  • Our program is designed for children starting from birth until 4th grade.
